About Me


I am a coffee and tea addict. Caffeine is love. Caffeine is life.

In my spare time you'll probably find me playing videogames, creating art, or reading. My fave games are Yakuza, Resident Evil, Shenmue, and Dragon Age.

My music tastes are all over the place. I'll go from listening to metal, to classical, to pop. I don't even care if I can't understand some of the musicians I listen to. Good music is good music. Sometimes I end up learning some vocab from different languages.

I'm not exactly the best cook. I can bake a bit, but I can't be trusted with fried foods. My last attempt at cooking a croquette yielded a sad and pathetic pile of charred dark matter. It was a mistake.

I have an irrational fear of butterflies. Yeah. They're very pretty to look at, but I freak out the moment they try to land on me. I spent a lot of my time trying to flee from them instead of admiring the pretty flowers in a butterfly garden once.
